About

I am a researcher focusing on atmospheric multiphase chemistry, secondary organic aerosols, and heterogeneous oxidation processes. My work combines laboratory experiments and field observations to better understand air pollution and climate interactions.

Research Interests

  • Multiphase atmospheric chemistry
  • Secondary organic aerosol formation
  • Field measurements and photochemical aging
  • Heterogeneous oxidation and kinetics
