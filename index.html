About

I study multiphase atmospheric chemistry with a focus on aqueous and heterogeneous oxidation pathways controlling HOx and peroxide budgets. My work bridges laboratory kinetic experiments, box models and analysis of field campaigns across urban and marine environments.

Research Interests

  • Multiphase oxidation (aqueous & heterogeneous)
  • HOx / H₂O₂ budgets and radical kinetics
  • Secondary organic aerosol formation & aging
  • Observation–model integration
